About John

John is a multi‑disciplinary designer and developer based in Orlando currently working at Envy and accepting select freelance projects. Between Envy and freelance, he helps plan, design and build modern web applications and websites for large brands and small businesses.

As a daily routine, John enjoys spending time with his growing family and riding his bicycle to and from work at Envy. Because cycling is such a passion of his, he enjoys taking on projects that directly—or indirectly —involve the great outdoors.

Scheduling

For the best chance at getting John to work on your project, you should try to book his time in advance. The minimum amount of hours required per project is 10 hours. You can find his available weeks and request to book his time using the grid below. Simply select the date you would like your project to start.

Rates

All work is billed at $125 per hour with consulting‑only services at a fixed price. If your project requires a fixed price, a non‑refundable down payment will be needed in order to reserve time. Otherwise, all time will need to be booked before project kickoff. There is minimum of 10 hours ($1250) per project.

Legal

All clients are be required to sign a Services Agreement. If an NDA is a requirement for the project, there will be an additional fee of $1250—the Services Agreement includes an NDA. View a sample Service Agreement

Availability

Simply click on an available time that you’d like to request a booking. If this format doesn’t work for you or your project is expected to begin at a later date than shown, simply write to john@j‑wilson.com and mention the date you would like work to begin.
