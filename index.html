Services

UI / UX Design

John's goal is to design applications and websites so that they are simple and easy to use. To make sure that details and interactions stay simple and easy to use, his process reflects that by being straightforward and refined while not overlooking important details and interactions.

Development

Whether you use John for your design work or someone else, he will make sure the simplicity of the original design is also transfered into the development phase by creating systems that are flexible and easily expandable. He uses MVCSS for front‑end and a DRY approach for back‑end development.

Consulting

Whether you have a mis‑matched brand, a new product idea, or an existing product that is stuggling to take off, John would love to help by appling his proven expreience to make sure you get everything on track. There are many different ways his consulting services can help you.

About John

John is a multi‑disciplinary designer and developer based in Orlando currently working at Envy and taking on select freelance projects. Between Envy and freelance, he helps plan, design and build modern web applications and websites for large brands and small businesses.
